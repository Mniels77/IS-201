My Life and Hobbies

Ubringing

I was born and raised in the small town of Nuevo Casas Grandes, Chihuauha, Mexico. My parents were also born and raised there. I have 4 siblings: Ben, Niki, David, and Sophie, and I have 2 in-law: Courtney and Dusty. My family and I are members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints.

Growing up in Mexico I was able to begin learning Spanish when I was about 6 years old. I attended and graduated Highschool in 2018 and served a 2 year mission in Kentucky from 2018 to 2020. After I returned home, I attended BYU-Provo, which is where I'm now working on a Bachelor's degree in Supply Chain Management.
